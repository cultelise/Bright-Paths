Endless Meandering

On and on and on and on and on and on and on.

Endlessly meandering. Never. Sometimes wrong.

Up and down and all around, we look but cannot find.

And when we go, we always know; we'll leave this world behind.

Silent seekers come and go.

They wait for us beyond.

Waiting. Seeking. Wandering.

Our children are all gone.

We know there's more that's yet in store.

The story doesn't end.

So when we would, if yet we could--

An ellipsis we append.
